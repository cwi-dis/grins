This page provides a gateway to the documentation for the GRiNS family of products. This gives you access to the most up-to-date version of all available manuals and guides.
 
GRiNS Templates Guides

The GRiNS Templates Guide gives a short introduction to each of the templates available in the various releases of GRiNS Editor products. The Templates Guide also explains how users of GRiNS Pro Editor for SMIL 2.0 can create new templates.


GRiNS How To Guides

The GRiNS How To Guides provide an overview of various aspects of GRiNS editing functionality. They provide general tips (such as background information on GRiNS techniques and editing shortcuts) and specific instructions for specialty tasks (such as associating HTML content in the RealOne player with your media window presentation).


GRiNS Demo Central

If you want examples of presentations created using GRiNS, visit GRiNS Demo Central. You will find demos made for RealOne, HTML+TIME, Mobile Multimedia Messaging (MMS) and other SMIL players.

All of the demos are packaged as self-contained zip files that may be downloaded to your machine. You may not reuse the assets, but feel free to be inspired to create you work work based on the possibilities enabled by various versions of GRiNS.
